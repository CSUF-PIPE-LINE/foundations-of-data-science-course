Data collected for all houses and condos within a reasonably small radius of Fullerton, CA listed for rent on redfin.com May 2-9, 2022.

Obvious data errors (e.g., a house with 0 bedrooms or 0 square feet) have already been removed.

City: the city corresponding to the house's street address

Price: the listed monthly rent

Beds: the listed number of bedrooms

Baths: the listed number of bathrooms

SqFt: the listed square footage of the house

Type: whether the housing unit was listed as a House or Condo

Location: OC = Orange County, LA = Los Angeles County, IE = Inland Empire (Riverside or San Bernardino County)
